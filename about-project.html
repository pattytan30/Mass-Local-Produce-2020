About Us

Project Background

As cities are reopening, people are eager to go out and loosen their indoor anxiety. Farm can be a good choice where people get low exposure to crowds but have fun with their friends and family. Within an hour of driving from Boston, various farms and local markets offer visitors to pick fruits and flowers, which enables them to make some connections with the nature.

Target Users

Young Adults & Family with kids

Pain Points
  • Users feel frustrated about finding farm activities.
  • Users barely know what produce is in the season.
  • Many farms' websites are boring and less user-friendly.
